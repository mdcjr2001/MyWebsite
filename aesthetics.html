WIGS

A manufactured covering of natural or synthetic hair for the head

Quick Weaves

A hair extension technique that involves gluing hair extensions to a cap or natural hair

Sew-Ins

A hair extension technique where wefts of hair are attached to your natural hair by sewing them onto cornrow braids created on your scalp

Faux Locs

A temporary hairstyle that uses synthetic hair extensions to create the look of locs on natural hair

Twists

A hairstyle that involves twisting two sections of hair around each other
